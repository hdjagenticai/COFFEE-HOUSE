About Us

Crafted for calm mornings, thoughtful work breaks, and unhurried evenings.

Coffee House was imagined as a refined pause in the rhythm of Dhantoli — a place where warm light, fresh coffee, and clean design come together naturally.

Set beside Yeshwant Stadium, our café blends premium coffee culture with everyday comfort. Whether you arrive for a solo espresso, a study session, or an easy catch-up with friends, the experience is designed to feel polished, inviting, and quietly memorable.

Our Mission

To serve quality coffee, comforting food, and attentive hospitality in a space that feels elevated without ever feeling formal — warm enough to relax in, and refined enough to return to often.

“We built Coffee House to feel personal. Whether you stop by for five quiet minutes or spend the afternoon here, we want you to feel looked after, relaxed, and genuinely welcome. Thank you for making us part of your day.”

— A warm note from the owner
